关于我们

HOPE是一个致力于减少世界上存在的性侵害案件的青年组织。我们为中小学生开设由专家反复审核的性教育课程，建立一个律师心理咨询师受害者的联动平台，更尝试通过研究向企业宣传性别平等文化的重要性……如果你也想为我们在做的事情尽一份力的话，欢迎继续阅读，你也会在文末找到我们的招聘链接。我们的大部分岗位可线上远程！

Empower child victims of sexual violence with free educational and legal survices

VISION - End all child sexual violence in the world

MISSION - HOPE's mission is to eliminate sexual violence among children and in workplaces. We achieve this by educating children, teachers and employees, providing post-abuse counseling platforms, and advocating for law and policy changes.

CLAIM - Empowerment, not Protection

HOPE怀光的故事

In 2018 , Won global Winner of Aurora Humanitarian Prize (with S3000 grant).

In 2021 , Led a national study of "status quo of anti-child sexual abuse education in China" by interviewing 3000+ students and teachers.

Learn more

我们的目标

1)weekly local teaching delivered by our professionally trained student lecturers

2) improving public policy through cooperating with local government to build comprehensive protecting system

3) training teachers in remote areas through online live teaching platform

4) conduct in-depth reasearch on this social issue through interviews and surveys with various social groups during our teachings, thus developing different solution sets to the issue depending on different targeted groups, and write informed and well-evidenced proposal to submit to National Congress urging for adding anti-child sexual abuse prevention education into the national curriculum.

Learn more

Measurement of Impact

Combining quantitative and qualitative data: quantity of children taught (quickly increasing the number means more potential sexual abuses are prevented) and quality of our education which is children’s true understanding of the content even after a long time, and ability to apply when actually facing danger, through our newly designed after-three-month and after-half-year surveys. The outcome is successful if the children do become more cautious and be able to apply the knowledge in dangerous situations if any. If not, we will also go back to redeliver classes for review purpose.

About HOPE

“HOPE” is a NGO founded in early 2017 by UWCCSC students and is composed of 50 dedicated members within five departments, to promote anti-sexual abuse education for children under 14 by directly delivering classes to local schools by our professionally trained “lecturers”, to raise social awareness of the issue by conducting social investigation, and to promote the legislative process. In 2018, we have established a cooperative relationship with Changshu government ensuring all 6-14 aged students in Changshu will be covered in 2 months and building a comprehensive protection system in Changshu on anti-child sexual abuse involving several governmental departments.
